About ElectroBits

Our Mission

ElectroBits revolutionizes overhead conductor monitoring for low voltage power lines through intelligent IoT sensors and real-time analysis. Our system prevents power outages by detecting obstacles, monitoring current flow, and identifying structural stress before they cause failures.

⚡ Current Monitoring

Arduino Uno with ADS-1115 ADC and SCT013 current transformer for precise current measurement up to 100A on overhead conductors.

📡 Wireless Communication

HC-05 Bluetooth module enables wireless data transmission to monitoring systems for real-time analysis and alerts.

🔍 Smart Detection

MPU-6050 accelerometer/gyroscope detects wire movement and vibrations, while active IR sensors identify obstacles like vegetation or debris.

Technical Specifications

  • Microcontroller: Arduino Uno (ATmega328P)
  • Current Sensor: SCT013 100A:50mA Non-invasive Current Transformer
  • ADC: ADS-1115 16-bit Analog-to-Digital Converter
  • Motion Detection: MPU-6050 6-axis Accelerometer/Gyroscope
  • Obstacle Detection: Active IR Sensor
  • Communication: HC-05 Bluetooth Module
  • Application: Overhead Low Voltage Conductor Monitoring

Key Features

Real-time Current Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of power flow through overhead conductors with 16-bit precision.

Movement Detection: Advanced motion sensing to detect excessive wire movement due to wind, structural issues, or external forces.

Obstacle Detection: IR-based detection system to identify vegetation growth, animal contact, or debris on power lines.

Wireless Monitoring: Bluetooth connectivity enables remote monitoring without physical access to power lines.
